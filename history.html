HERITAGE & LEGACY

The Origins of Excellence

Founded in 1909 by Wilhelm Maybach, one of the most influential engineers in automotive history, the Maybach brand was conceived with a singular vision: to create machines defined by refinement, precision and timeless engineering values.

A Name Synonymous with Prestige

From airship engines to handcrafted luxury automobiles, Maybach quickly became a symbol of technical mastery and elite craftsmanship. Ownership was never about excess — it was about understanding quality at its highest level.

Rebirth Through Innovation

Under the Mercedes-Benz legacy, Maybach was reborn with a renewed purpose: preserve its heritage while embracing modern innovation. The result is a brand that blends historical prestige with contemporary luxury.

The Philosophy of Modern Luxury

Today, Maybach represents a sanctuary in motion. Every detail is intentional, every surface crafted to communicate serenity, confidence and authority without compromise.

As Origens da Excelência

Fundada em 1909 por Wilhelm Maybach, um dos engenheiros mais influentes da história automóvel, a marca Maybach nasceu com uma visão singular: criar máquinas definidas por refinamento, precisão e valores de engenharia intemporais.

Um Nome Sinónimo de Prestígio

Desde motores para dirigíveis até automóveis de luxo artesanais, a Maybach tornou-se rapidamente um símbolo de mestria técnica e artesanato de elite. Possuir um Maybach nunca foi sobre excesso, mas sim sobre compreensão do verdadeiro luxo.
